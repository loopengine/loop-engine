## Maintainer responsibilities

- Review and merge PRs within 5 business days
- Triage new issues within 3 business days
- Publish releases on the agreed cadence
- Uphold the Code of Conduct

## Becoming a maintainer

Consistent contributors who demonstrate deep understanding of the project's
design principles may be nominated as maintainers.

Process:
1. Nomination by an existing maintainer (open an issue with `[Maintainer nomination]` in the title)
2. 2/3 majority approval from the current maintainer group
3. Announcement in CHANGELOG.md and updated MAINTAINERS.md
